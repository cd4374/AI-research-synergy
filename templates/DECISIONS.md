# Decision Log

> All pivot, scope, priority, and strategic decisions. Every decision
> records the rationale and what evidence/reviews led to it.

## Format

```markdown
## Decision: DEC-{seq}
- **Date**: {date}
- **Type**: pivot | scope_change | priority_change | termination | escalation
- **Made by**: /ars-01-coordinator | human
- **Reason**: {why this decision was made}
- **Based on**: {review IDs, evidence IDs, or human input}
- **Old plan**: {what was previously planned}
- **New plan**: {what changed}
- **Tasks affected**: {task IDs cancelled, created, or modified}
- **Status**: proposed | adopted | rejected
```
